Gladius

GLADIUS, the 2022 98ft (30.12m) Ferretti, is an excellent example of a modern yacht comprising impressive alfresco areas, sumptuous accommodations and a stunning main salon built around fun and relaxation. She showcases sleek, clean lines on her exterior while her interior boasts contemporary furnishings. GLADIUS is fast, spacious, and beautiful, making her the perfect yacht for an entertainer to host family and friends while exploring the islands. GLADIUS is under full factory warranty until December 2022. Guests can board the yacht via the aft deck or the swim platform.
