Ferrari SF90 Stradale

The Ferrari SF90 Stradale is an impressive hybrid supercar that combines a 4.0-liter V8 engine with three electric motors, delivering a mind-boggling total power output of 986 horsepower. With its advanced hybrid system, the SF90 Stradale can reach a top speed of 211 mph, making it one of the fastest road-legal Ferraris ever produced. The car features an intelligent all-wheel drive system that maximizes traction and handling, providing exceptional driving dynamics. Accelerating from 0 to 60 mph in just 2.5 seconds, the SF90 Stradale showcases remarkable acceleration capabilities. Its aerodynamic design, including active aerodynamic elements, ensures optimal downforce and stability at high speeds. With an electric-only range of 16 miles, the SF90 Stradale also offers the option of silent and emission-free driving in urban areas.
