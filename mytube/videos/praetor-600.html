Praetor 600

The Praetor 600 impresses with an impressive range of 4,018 nautical miles, enabling nonstop transatlantic flights, such as New York to London. Its advanced avionics system, including the cutting-edge Garmin G5000 flight deck, provides pilots with enhanced situational awareness and precise navigation capabilities. With a top speed of Mach 0.83, the Praetor 600 offers efficient and time-saving travel. The spacious cabin can comfortably accommodate up to 12 passengers, offering luxurious seating and customizable interior options. The Praetor 600 features the industry-first Active Turbulence Reduction technology, providing a smoother and more comfortable flight experience. Its large windows and advanced soundproofing contribute to a quiet and enjoyable cabin environment.
