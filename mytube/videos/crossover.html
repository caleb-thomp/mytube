Crossover

From the Antonini Navi shipyard in the Gulf of La Spezia comes the 41.9m Crossover, a superyacht with Mediterranean flair whose make-up is a modern reinterpretation of classic, Italian style. Designed with simplicity and elegance for a precise function, this motor yacht is full of personality and offers an incredible connection with the sea. The versatile and fluid architecture, which offers a broad level of client customisation, provides a terrace on the water and a way to enjoy life at sea in a whole new fashion.
